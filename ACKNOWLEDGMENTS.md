# Acknowledgments

**A Survey and Computational Atlas of Personality Models**
*Raetano, Gregor, & Tamang (2026)*

This project stands on the shoulders of over a century of personality science, open-source software, and cloud infrastructure. We gratefully acknowledge the researchers, developers, and organizations whose work made this atlas possible.
